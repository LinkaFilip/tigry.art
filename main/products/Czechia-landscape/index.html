Details

All of our cards have been mindfully made and printed in Melbourne. We work with the best local artisans and suppliers to craft your gift. Each card is intentional and comes with unique textural finishes such as embossing, blind deboss, foil and letterpress. Our stocks are premium and weighty - there is nothing flimsy about us. We are cards made to last, cards to be kept and treasured.

Short Talk cards measure 5.5inches x 4.25inches and come with a premium paper vellum envelope meaning they are slightly sheer so you can see through to the word detailing on the front of the card.

SUSTAINABILITY

We recognise that we are a paper-based product so we try our utmost wherever possible to limit impact to the environment. Our stocks are selected based on their environmental impact (eg recycled coffee cups) and we make them to last - our hope is that they are treasured forever. Our packaging is also sourced carefully for its sustainability properties. This is a work in progress for us and we will continue to uncover better ways of minimising any environmental impact. We're not perfect, but we are certainly trying to be.
